Cursor is set to pointer in gameplay mode. It is disabled when game is to end.
Human player character is set to 'O' while the aiPlayer's is set to 'X. The human player move is indicated by 'O' and that of aiPlayer by X.
A successful combination renders differnt colors in differnt cases to indicate the outcome of the game.
If human player wins, the successful combinaition cells having 'O' are rendered with blue background.
If aiPlayer wins, the the successful combination, the corresponding combination in this outcome of the game, is depicted by 'X' cells with red background color.
An outcome of tie makes all cells green.
